# **App Name**: Campus Hub

## Core Features:

- Unified Login: Single sign-on for both the intranet and virtual classroom using Firebase Authentication.
- Landing Page: A central landing page after login with buttons/icons for easy navigation to the intranet and virtual classroom.
- Intranet Portal: Dedicated section for internal communications, announcements, and resources. Features could include news feed, document sharing, and employee directory.
- Virtual Classroom: Environment for online courses, assignments, and interactive learning. Includes video lectures, collaborative tools, and grade tracking.
- AI Content Summarizer: An AI-powered tool that will summarize the intranet communications.
- File Storage: Cloud-based storage for documents, lecture recordings, and assignments.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color (Accent): Red (#D92332 or #BF0A30) for main buttons, key alerts, and high-importance graphics.
- Background color/Base: White or Light Gray (#FFFFFF or #F4F4F4) for the general intranet background and dashboard cards.
- Secondary color (Text and Elements): Dark Gray or Soft Black (#333333 or #555555) to ensure legibility.
- Additional Accent Color (Success/Progress): Vibrant Green or a calming blue (#4CAF50 or #007BFF) to indicate success or general information.
- Headline font: 'Poppins', sans-serif, for headings and short text with a modern feel.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, for comfortable reading of longer text.
- Use custom-designed icons with a line art style that complement the overall fresh and innovative aesthetic.
- Employ a card-based layout to organize content effectively, complemented by clean, minimal whitespace to maintain an open and uncluttered presentation.
- Incorporate subtle transitions and hover effects to enrich user interactions without overwhelming the design.
